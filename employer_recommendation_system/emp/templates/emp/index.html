About Us

The Spoken Tutorial Project is a Massive Open Online Courses or MOOCs based IT-software training project of the National Mission on Education through ICT, funded by Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.
Topper students from Spoken Tutorial Tests will be able to apply for the jobs via Job Recommendation System.
